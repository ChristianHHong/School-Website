Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

Our school church is an Evangelical Baptist multi-site megachurch, based in Lake Forest, California. It is one of the largest churches in California, and is among the largest in the United States of America. The church has several campuses in California and around the world. Weekly church attendance was 32,500 people in 2022.



school library

Was built in 1980

Our library includes a collection of books, and possibly other materials and media, that is accessible for use by its members and members of allied institutions. We also provide physical (hard copies) or digital (soft copies) materials, and may be a physical location, a virtual space, or both. Our library's collection normally includes printed materials which may be borrowed, and usually also includes a reference section of publications which may only be utilized inside the premises.



school auditorium

Was built in 1982

Our auditorium is a giant room built to enable a school audience to hear and watch performances. For us, the number of auditoria (or auditoriums) is expressed as the number of screens. Auditoria can be found in entertainment venues, community halls, and theaters, and may be used for rehearsal, presentation, performing arts productions, or as a learning space all around school campus.
